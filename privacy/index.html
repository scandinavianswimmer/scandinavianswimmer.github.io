Privacy Policy

DATEMAXXING uses account data, live capture, private AI scoring, and safety systems to run an 18+ dating app. This page explains the data we collect, why we use it, who processes it, and how users can control it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email, phone number, date of birth, username, and preferences.
  • Capture and scoring data: live face photo, capture metadata, and your private aesthetics score and report. The current v1 service does not create or retain a face template or facial embedding.
  • Profile content: profile photos, prompts, answers, and profile settings.
  • Messages: chat text exchanged with matches.
  • Location: coarse location used for geographically relevant matches.
  • Device and technical data: device identifiers, app version, IP address, diagnostics, and security events.
  • Safety and privacy records: reports, blocks, moderation events, consent records, data exports, deletion records, and biometric revocation records.

2. How We Use Information

  • Create and secure accounts.
  • Confirm users are 18+ and eligible to use the service.
  • Confirm that a scoring photo was taken with the in-app camera.
  • Generate private, self-only aesthetics scores and score reports.
  • Enforce in-range matching, likes, Roses, reports, blocks, and chat.
  • Moderate messages and reports for user safety.
  • Provide data export, biometric consent revoke, and account deletion workflows.

We do not sell biometric data. Providers may not use DATEMAXXING user data for advertising, resale, or any purpose outside the contracted service.

4. Automated Scoring

DATEMAXXING uses an AI-assisted system to compute a private aesthetics score from a face photograph. The score is visible only to the user and is used for score reports, coaching, and matching eligibility. It is not shown on public profiles and is not a medical, psychological, credit, employment, housing, education, or legally determinative assessment.

5. Retention

  • Raw face capture: designed to be destroyed within 24 hours after scoring completes.
  • Face templates or facial embeddings: the current v1 service does not create or retain them.
  • Account, profile, messages, and match records: retained while the account is active, then deleted or de-identified according to deletion workflow and legal obligations.
  • Safety, fraud, and legal records: retained as needed to protect users, investigate abuse, and comply with law.

6. Your Controls

  • Download My Data: request a machine-readable export in Settings.
  • Revoke Biometric Consent: remove hosted capture storage while keeping your profile, when available.
  • Delete Account: delete your account and associated data, subject to legal and safety exceptions.
  • Report or Block: report safety issues and block users from app surfaces.

7. Children

DATEMAXXING is only for users 18 or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we learn that an underage user created an account, we will suspend the account and delete associated data as required.
